Website Planning Document

RG Built Corp.

Site Name

RG Built Corp.

This name represents a construction focused website that showcases general construction services including architectural works, civil works, and MEPF. I chose this name because it sounds professional but still personal to me as someone working in the construction industry while studying software development.

Optional domain availability: rgbuiltcorp.com

Logo

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about construction services, completed projects, and basic construction solutions for residential and commercial clients.

  • Information about general construction services
  • Sample construction projects
  • Construction scope such as architectural, electrical, plumbing, and mechanical works
  • Company background
  • Contact form for inquiries
  • Gallery of project photos

Scenarios

  • What construction services does RG Built Corp. offer?
  • How can I contact the company for quotation and project inquiries?
  • Can I see sample finished projects before hiring the contractor?
  • Does the company handle electrical, plumbing, and air conditioning works too?
